About me

My name is Maxime Heinsbroek and I'm currently a Communication and Multimedia student at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. I was born on the 27th of May 1995 in Hilversum, the Netherlands, and now based in Amsterdam. I speak Dutch, English, HTML, CSS and Javascript – the last three languages I use predominantly for web development and webdesign. In addition, I have skills in Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign. Besides being a little bit nerdy, I spend my time modeling and enjoying Amsterdam with friends.
